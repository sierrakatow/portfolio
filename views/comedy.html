who does this girl think she is?

Sierra Katow is a half-Chinese American, half-Japanese American comic who first began performing standup when she was 16 years old at various comedy clubs around the LA area. She is currently a junior attending Harvard College where she continues to do standup in and around Boston. On campus, she performs with the Harvard Standup Comic Society. She performs at the Comedy Studio in Cambridge, the Hollywood Comedy Store, the Pasadena Ice House, and the Flappers Comedy Club in Burbank. During the summer months when on break from college, she performs weekly at the Hollywood Laugh Factory.

Sierra's standup has been featured on 2 episodes of Laugh on Fox. She has also been showcased in the LA IO West Comedy Festival.
