Sierra Katow is a half-Chinese American, half-Japanese American comic who first began performing standup when she was 16 years old at various comedy clubs around the LA area. She is currently in her third year at Harvard College, studying Computer Science, where she continues to do standup in and around Boston. On campus, she performs with the Harvard Standup Comic Society.

Sierra's standup has been featured as a comedy spotlight on Last Call with Carson Daly on NBC and on 2 episodes of Laugh on Fox. She has also been showcased in the LA IO West Comedy Festival.
